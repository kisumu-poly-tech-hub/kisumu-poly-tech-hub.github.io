Robotics

The robotics contest which is an annual event and has been successfully held for the last nine (9) years is one of the means by which technology and innovation is promoted within TVET institutions and universities. It is therefore, crucial that trainees and university students are fully prepared from the onset of the robot constraction so that they can translate knowledge acquired in class into practical application.

It is for this reason that the robot contest rules have been prepared to enable adequate preparations and full involvement of trainees, university students and lectures. The competition will involve regional and national contests whose date will be communicated later.
